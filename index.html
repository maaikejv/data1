Context

The idea: Amsterdam paralympics 2024

The City of Amsterdam wants to host a 5km. open water swimming event through the canals of Amsterdam. On behalf of the municipality of Amsterdama proposal is made on the feasibility of the event from the perspective of the safety of the partaking athletes from an environmental perspective. The event is going to be hosted in May 2024.

The assignments

With the following assignments, the feasibility and conditions of this event were tested and advise to give an advise.

ASSIGNMENT 1: WATER

Water is an essential resource that is crucial for the survival of all living organisms, including humans. In the city, water plays a vital role in ensuring proper sanitation and hygiene. It is used for drinking, cooking, cleaning, and various other household activities. Additionally, water is also used in industries such as manufacturing, construction, and transportation.


In urban areas, the demand for water is much higher than in rural areas, making it important to have proper water management systems in place. These systems include water treatment plants, pipelines, and reservoirs that ensure a steady supply of clean water to the city's inhabitants.


Mooore text

Furthermore, the conservation of water resources is also crucial in cities, where the population is more concentrated, and the effects of water scarcity can be felt more acutely. It is therefore important that individuals and businesses in the city take steps to reduce water wastage and implement water-saving measures in their daily activities.


In conclusion, water is a vital resource that is essential for the survival and well-being of city dwellers. Proper water management, conservation, and responsible usage are crucial to ensure a sustainable and healthy future for urban communities.
